Only the ₹29 Monthly Plan is refundable, and only if requested within 24 hours of payment. The ₹1 trial is a token activation amount and is non-refundable.

To request a refund, scroll down to the box below, select "Refund Request", describe your reason, and submit — we'll get back to you.

Payment is simple and manual via UPI:

  • Tap your plan (₹1 Trial or ₹29 Monthly) to open the payment sheet
  • Scan the QR code or pay using the UPI ID shown, via any UPI app
  • Enter your Transaction ID and upload a screenshot of the payment
  • Tap Submit Payment — your plan shows "Under Review"
  • Once verified (usually a few hours), Pro activates automatically — live, no refresh needed

First, try submitting a fresh payment request — double-check your Transaction ID and screenshot are correct and clearly visible.
